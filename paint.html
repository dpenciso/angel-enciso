Paint

Quality is His Business

A few years after moving to Utah, Angel decided to start his own painting company called The Painting Business.
Founded in 1998, The Painting Business is dedicated to providing service that is second to none. Amongst the services offered are:

  • Interior and Exterior
  • Residential and Commercial
  • New Builds and Remodels
  • Wood Finishes (including cabinetry and shiplap)
  • Faux Finish
  • AND MUCH MORE!

The Painting Business has painted edifices such as:

  1. The Sao Paulo Brazil LDS Temple
  2. The Lima Peru LDS Temple
  3. The Stephen Covey Residence
  4. The American Towers in Salt Lake City
  5. The Larry King Residence
